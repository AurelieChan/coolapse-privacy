Privacy Policy – Coolapse

Last updated: 2026-02-26

Summary

Coolapse stores your game progress locally on your device. We do not collect personal data directly. The app may display ads via Google AdMob, which may process device information to provide advertising.

Data We Collect

We do not ask for your name, email, or account, and we do not collect personal data directly on our servers.

Local Storage

Your game progress, settings, and scores are stored locally on your device only. Removing the app or clearing its storage may delete them.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Coolapse may show advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK). AdMob and its partners may process information such as device identifiers, approximate location, and interactions with ads in order to deliver and measure ads.

  • Ads may be personalized (based on your interests) or non-personalized.
  • In the EEA/UK/Switzerland, the app may display a consent message when required.
  • You can typically change your choice later via the app’s privacy settings (if available) or your device settings.

Your Choices

  • Consent (EEA/UK/CH): when shown, you can accept or refuse personalized ads.
  • iOS: you can control app tracking permissions in system settings (App Tracking Transparency).
  • Android: you can control your advertising/privacy settings in system settings.

Third-Party Policies

For details about Google’s advertising and data practices, please refer to Google’s policies: Google Privacy Policy.
